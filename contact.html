Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Do you offer customization services?

Yes, we specialize in custom jewelry design. You can schedule a consultation with our design team to discuss your vision, whether it's modifying an existing design or creating something entirely new from scratch.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all non-customized items in their original condition with all certificates and packaging. Custom pieces are final sale unless there is a manufacturing defect. All returns are processed within 5-7 business days.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our contact form, by calling our boutique directly, or by emailing appointments@luxegems.com. We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for design consultations.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All international shipments are fully insured and may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

How do I care for my jewelry?

We recommend removing jewelry before swimming, showering, or applying cosmetics. Store pieces separately in soft pouches to prevent scratching. For cleaning, use a soft cloth and mild soap. We also offer complimentary annual cleaning and inspection services.
