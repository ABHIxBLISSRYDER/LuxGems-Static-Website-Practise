Our Story

Crafting timeless elegance since 1995

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Founded in the heart of New York City, LuxeGems began as a small family workshop with a singular vision: to create jewelry that transcends trends and becomes cherished heirlooms. For over 25 years, our master artisans have dedicated themselves to the ancient art of jewelry making, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design.

Each piece in our collection is born from a passion for excellence and an unwavering commitment to quality. We believe that true luxury lies in the details—the perfect setting, the precise cut, the harmonious balance of elements that transforms precious materials into wearable art.

Our Ethical Commitment

At LuxeGems, beauty is more than skin deep. We are committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices throughout our supply chain. All our diamonds are conflict-free, certified by the Kimberley Process, and our precious metals are sourced from responsible mines that prioritize environmental stewardship and fair labor practices.

We work closely with artisanal mining communities to ensure fair wages and safe working conditions, believing that true luxury should uplift everyone it touches—from the miner who extracts the raw materials to the customer who wears the finished piece.
